Trained as a Digital Artist specializing in 3D animation and modeling. B.A. in Animation and Digital Arts from the Tecnológico de Monterrey (Mexico). M.A. in Game Development and Research in TH Köln (Germany).

Proficient user in Autodesk Maya, Blender, Unity, Unreal. Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects. Polyglot by nature: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, HTML, Java, C++, C# MEL, Python, Linden Scripting Language and Latin.

Worked freelance since 2005 in virtual photography and digital art. Skilled in rapid prototyping and exploring futuristic ideas. Additional experience and training teaching computer design and plastic arts in large groups as well as one-on-one.

Oh, and the tagline above wasn't a joke. I am a baritone with a penchant for arie di bravura.
